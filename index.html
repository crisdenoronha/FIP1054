LONDON, ONTARIO - CANADA

London, Ontario, which is proudly known as The Forest City, is home to nearly 400,000 residents. It is a hub for higher education, medical research, manufacturing, and technology.

The city is rich in heritage, arts, cultural spaces and natural beauty. Whether you enjoy walking in beautiful parks or along the banks of the Thames River, or if you prefer to visit one of our 14 local museums or catch a world-class performance, London offers all the amenities found in much larger cities.
